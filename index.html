About me

I'm a UX/UI designer with 4 years of experience creating digital products across mobile, web, and IoT. I'm based in Belgrade, Serbia, working with clients remotely and locally.

My background in psychology gives me a genuine interest in how people think and make decisions — which I find endlessly useful when designing interfaces. I care about the product, not just the screen.

I write about my design process on Medium (in English) and Habr (in Russian), because I believe good design thinking deserves to be shared and discussed.
